About me

I'm an indie developer. My nickname is Petrixx1. At first, my name was going to be Petrix.

But because scratch and itch.io (more on that later) wouldn't let me call myself Petrix,

they offered me the name "Petrixx1" and I accepted that. My first steps were in Scratch.

I found out about itch.io when I was looking for a new

platform where I could put my 2 games: Star-rise and Flappy Submarine.

They were made in Scratch. I'm not making only games.

I am making also comics, but I am still not publishing any my comics.
